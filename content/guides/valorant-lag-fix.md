date: 2024-05-06
description: "Optimization tips for smoother gameplay."
---
Lag can be the difference between a headshot and a whiff. Follow these steps to optimize your network and PC settings for Valorant.

## 1. Network Settings
- Use a wired connection (Ethernet).
- Disable "Network Buffering" in game settings.

## 2. PC Optimization
- Turn on "Nvidia Reflex Low Latency".
- Set your power plan to "High Performance".
